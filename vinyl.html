Vinyls!

I love collecting vinyls! I recently (around a year ago) bought a vinyl player and have started collecting vinyls! Although I absolutely love vinyls, the one thing that I dislike is that you have to listen to the whole album rather than being able to listen to one song on repeat (spotify withdrawals). This is great for some albums! But for others, I only like a single song... But I definitely think its a cool way to support the artist and I love having physical media! Below, I have listed some of my current favorite albums (my music taste changes often and its relatively diverse? (I will listen to almost anything!) But for now I am definitely in an indie/alt music phase! The one thing that genuinely breaks my heart is that one of my favorite artists (Solya) released two vinyls; one of them was a heart and the other one was purple (super cute!) but they sold out T-T and they aren't coming back because they are limited edition (My heart is broken < / 3) Also, quick shoutout to Mars Argo and Daisy and the Scouts (hopefully a new album from them soon? (hopefully wishing))... But you never know! Anyways if you have not listened to any of these artists, definitely check them out!
